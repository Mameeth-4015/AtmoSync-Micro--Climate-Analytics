Project 1 - "AtmoSync": Micro-Climate Arbitrage Analytics
Problem Statement: Traditional supply chain analytics rely on standard transit times and macro-weather
forecasts. They fail to track how real-time, hyper-local micro-climate shifts (e.g., sudden humidity spikes
inside a specific shipping container) degrade agricultural commodities before they reach the market.
Use Case: A commodities trader at Infotact monitors the AtmoSync dashboard. The streaming pipeline
detects that Container A's internal temperature has drifted, increasing the spoilage rate of the avocados
inside. The dashboard instantly calculates "Spoilage Arbitrage" and alerts the trader to reroute the shipment
to a closer, secondary market—securing a premium price before the goods degrade below quality thresholds.
